About Us

We are dedicated to transforming water management with cutting-edge technology, making it smarter, more efficient, and sustainable for all.

Our Story

At Waba Yangu, we're on a mission to bring smart water metering solutions to communities and businesses. Our advanced technologies aim to make water usage more efficient, transparent, and cost-effective for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities and businesses with advanced water metering systems that reduce waste, enhance sustainability, and drive cost savings. We integrate technology to help users manage water more effectively.

Our Vision

We aspire to be leaders in the digital transformation of water management, improving resource conservation and creating value for users. We envision a world where smart water management is the standard for everyone.

Our Core Values

We operate with integrity, innovation, and sustainability at the heart of everything we do.

Integrity

We prioritize honesty, transparency, and trust in all our interactions with customers, partners, and communities.

Innovation

We embrace cutting-edge technology and continuously seek innovative solutions to enhance water management practices.

Sustainability

We are committed to promoting sustainable water usage practices and contributing to environmental conservation for future generations.

Waba Yangu's Timeline

Since our inception, Waba Yangu has been committed to revolutionizing water management through smart metering solutions. Our journey began in 2015 with the vision to address water wastage and billing inaccuracies till today.

Launch of Waba Yangu

Waba Yangu was officially launched, marking the start of our mission to transform water management systems. We introduced our first water metering solutions, designed to help communities and businesses reduce wastage and manage water usage efficiently.

First Digital Water Meters Deployed

We deployed our first batch of digital water meters, which allowed customers to monitor their water usage in real-time, contributing to more accurate billing and resource management.

Expansion to New Regions

Waba Yangu expanded its operations to several new regions, enabling more communities to access our smart water management systems. This move significantly increased our customer base and impact.

Introduction of Prepaid Water Meters
